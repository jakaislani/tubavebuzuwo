Aleza (Rapper) Age, Biography, Net Worth, Height, Boyfriend, Daughter

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Aleza is an American rapper repping both Memphis and Mississippi. She is popularly known for being the talent behind the songs “You The Type,” and “Emotional,” and her collaborations with K-Carbon, Slimeroni, on Gloss Up song “Shabooya.’

In this post, we will be looking at the upcoming Memphis rapper’s biography and other important details about her. Before, we proceed let’s start by looking at her profile summary in the table below.

Profile Summary

Aleza Biography

Aleza who is also known as Aleghsa Haywood was born on January 2, 1998, in Mississippi, USA. The 25-year-old rapper is also known to her fans as Aleghsa Jamel.

Further research shows that our upcoming rapper has yet to share information about her parents and siblings with the public. The same also goes for her educational background.

Aleza Career

Aleza is becoming well-known as one of the top female hip-hop artists in Memphis. Her journey to fame is as unique as her style.

Following in the footsteps of the rapper Glorilla, she has stood out with her smooth flow and music that’s inspired by R&B, making her different from the others in the crowded hip-hop scene.

Although she’s connected to Glorilla’s success, Aleza is not living in anyone’s shadow. She’s just as determined and ambitious as the big names in the music industry.

Her music has attracted more than 22k monthly listeners on Spotify. She also has 19.3k subscribers on YouTube and a significant following of over 183k on Instagram.

On her Instagram account, you can get a personal look into her life. She shares clips from her concerts, personal photos, moments with her family, and promotional content.

Her posts also reflect her love for fashion, faith, and the finer things in life, which resonates with her 184k followers.

Her YouTube channel is a goldmine for music lovers. It features music videos and behind-the-scenes vlogs that give fans an exclusive peek into her creative process.

Additionally, her Twitter account, “Only1aleza,” is where she shares her thoughts, opinions, and interacts with her audience through retweets, forming a more personal connection with her growing fan base.

Also, information obtained from Spotify says she has only released one album which was in 2019. The album is called “Aleza the Ape” and it contains 7 songs with a total runtime of 19 min 38 sec.

Over the years she has also released hit songs such as “You The Type,” “Whatever,” “Skin Tone,” “Emotional,” and many more.

Relationship Status & Boyfriend

The 5-foot-7-inch tall rapper is currently dating her boyfriend Tezzo Santana. They already have a daughter together named Malaya. Malaya was born in December of 2022.

Net Worth

Aleza’s has an estimated net worth of $500k to $1 million. Her primary source of income is her music. Nevertheless, she also earns when people stream her songs on music streaming platforms like Spotify, Apple Music, and the like.

Additionally, money is also made from music concerts, brands & sponsorship partnerships as well as from her YouTube channel through ads.
