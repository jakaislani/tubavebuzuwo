Ty Pennington Married To Girlfriend Kellee Merrell| Insights About His Net Worth &amp; Salary

August 2024 · 3 minute read

The 56-year-old TV presenter, Ty Pennington, married his quarantine partner, Kellee Merrell, at their home in Savannah, Georgia, which is currently under construction. According to People, the pair tied the knot in front of eight of their closest friends and family members on Saturday, 27 November. They held their small wedding ceremony in a 19th-century house restored by Pennington and Merrell, which is still under renovation.

The TV star was in a relationship with his longtime girlfriend, Andrea Bock, for more than two decades. They started dating back in 1996. However, they didn’t get married and kept things simple as they were satisfied with their state. They also didn’t share any kids together. The two separated in 2009. His ex-girlfriend attended Sprayberry High School and graduated from the Savannah College of Art and Design with a Bachelors' degree in Theatre Arts and Design in 1968. She worked with Pennington as his personal assistant and a manager. 

And one year later, the TV presenter met his now-wife, Kellee, for the first time in 2010 but didn’t strike up a romance. However, they grew their connection with each other and finally reconnected in early 2020. They spent quarantine together and used it as a chance to take their relationship even further. He said, “Luckily our paths finally crossed at the right time. It’s one of those things where you just feel happy being around a person. I never thought I would get married but she changed that. Glad I waited for the one.”

Ty Pennington announced his engagement with Kellee Merrell in July 2021 on Instagram by posting a picture of their rings and captioning, “It’s the yes for me.”

The picture showed the engagement ring was a large teardrop-shaped diamond bauble.

His wife, Kelle Merrell, is a social media manager who works managing a brand’s presence on social platforms. She hails from the city of Vancouver, Canada.
