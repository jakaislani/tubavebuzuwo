Plotlines and Story

Kidnapped Bride is a manhwa webtoon series that follows the story of Lucina, a young woman who was born as the illegitimate daughter of Lord Berek, a powerful and cruel nobleman. Lucina lived a miserable life as a maid in her father’s castle, where she was abused and mistreated by him and his wife. Lucina’s only hope was to escape from her hellish existence, and find her true love.

One day, Lucina’s wish came true, but not in the way she expected. She was kidnapped by Hakkan, a mysterious and handsome man who claimed to be the great conqueror of the dragon lands of Tayer. Hakkan offered Lucina a deal: he would spare her father’s life, if she agreed to be his bride. Lucina accepted the deal, hoping to find a better life with Hakkan.

However, Lucina soon realized that Hakkan was not an ordinary man, but a powerful and feared dragon lord, who had a dark and dangerous past. Hakkan was also not interested in Lucina as a bride, but as a tool for his plans. Hakkan wanted to use Lucina’s blood, which contained a rare and ancient power, to awaken his true dragon form, and to conquer the world.

Lucina found herself in a dilemma: she had to choose between her father, who hated her, and Hakkan, who used her. She also had to deal with the consequences of her decision, as she became involved in a war between humans and dragons, and a prophecy that foretold her fate. Lucina had to face many challenges and enemies, as well as discover many secrets and mysteries, along the way.

But Lucina also found something unexpected: love. She developed feelings for Hakkan, who also showed signs of caring for her. Lucina and Hakkan formed a bond that transcended their differences and conflicts. They also learned to trust and respect each other, and to overcome their fears and doubts. Lucina and Hakkan became partners and lovers, who shared a destiny and a dream.
